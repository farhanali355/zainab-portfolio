What is Local SEO?

Local Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of optimizing your online presence to attract more business from relevant local searches. These searches take place on Google and other search engines.

Why Local SEO Matters

With over 46% of all Google searches seeking local information, optimizing for local search is essential for any business with a physical location or serving a specific geographical area.

76%
of people who search on their smartphone for something nearby visit a business within a day
88%
of local searches on a mobile device result in either a call or visit
28%
of local searches result in a purchase

Google's Local Pack

The Local Pack (or "Snack Pack") appears at the top of search results for local queries and shows the top 3 local businesses. Getting into the Local Pack can increase your visibility by 500%.

Appear in the top 3 results for local searches
Show up on Google Maps with directions
Display star ratings and reviews directly in search

Key Elements of Local SEO

Google My Business (GMB)

Your GMB profile is the most important local SEO factor. It appears in Google Maps and local search results, showing your business information, photos, reviews, and posts.

Complete Profile Regular Posts Photos & Videos Q&A Management

Local Citations

Citations are online mentions of your business name, address, and phone number (NAP). Consistency across directories like Yelp, Yellow Pages, and industry-specific sites is crucial.

NAP Consistency Directory Listings Industry Directories Local Chambers

Online Reviews

Positive reviews build trust and improve local rankings. The quantity, quality, and recency of reviews all impact your visibility in local search results.

Review Generation Review Responses Review Monitoring Star Ratings

Local Content

Creating content specific to your local area shows Google you're a relevant, authoritative source for local searches. This includes local news, events, and area-specific information.

Local Blog Posts Event Coverage Area Guides Community News
